MEDICAL CODING & BILLING
MEDICAL TRANSCRIPTION
MEDICAL SCRIBING

Medical coding is a fundamental healthcare process that involves the conversion of intricate medical diagnostics, treatments, processes, and services into uniform alphanumeric identifiers. These identifiers are employed for a variety of purposes, including billing, the processing of insurance claims, and the preservation of medical records. A highly skilled medical coder is essential for the successful and efficient management of healthcare operations, as they must have a thorough comprehension of medical terminology, anatomical anatomy, and healthcare regulations in order to assign the relevant codes that accurately reflect the patient's state and the services rendered. The accuracy of medical coding is critical for the receipt of appropriate reimbursement, compliance with regulatory regulations, and the exchange of patient information throughout the healthcare system.

Duration : 3 Months
Qualification : Degree / Diploma
Mode : Online & Offline

Medical transcription is an essential healthcare service that involves the conversion of audio recordings of patient conversations, medical examinations, and the dictation of healthcare professionals into written text. Highly qualified medical transcriptionists, commonly referred to as Healthcare Documentation Specialists (HDSI), are responsible for ensuring the accuracy and veracity of medical records. HDSI must have a thorough comprehension of medical terminology, anatomical anatomy, and the subtleties of healthcare documentation. The accuracy of medical transcription is necessary for the preservation of clear and complete patient records, the facilitation of communication between healthcare providers, and the support of billing and insurance procedures. Medical transcriptionists are employed in a variety of healthcare settings, such as hospitals, clinics, or transcription service companies, and use technology and knowledge to transcribe and modify medical documents in accordance with strict confidentiality and regulatory requirements. Their work is essential for the provision of quality patient care and the efficient management of healthcare operations.

Duration : 4 Months
Qualification : Plus Two/ Diploma / Deg ree
Mode : Online & Offline
